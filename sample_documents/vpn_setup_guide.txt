TechNova Solutions VPN Setup Guide
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Document Owner: IT Department

1. OVERVIEW

This guide explains how to install and configure SecureConnect, TechNova's
VPN client, which is required to access internal systems, shared drives, and
customer data from outside a company office, as described in the Remote Work
Policy.

2. PREREQUISITES

Before installing SecureConnect, confirm the following:
  - You are using a company-issued laptop (personal devices are not
    supported and cannot be enrolled)
  - Your laptop is connected to the internet and fully updated
  - You know your company email address and network password
  - You have enrolled in multifactor authentication (MFA) through the
    company Identity Portal; if not, see Section 6 before continuing

3. INSTALLATION ON WINDOWS

  1. Open the Company Software Center from the Start menu.
  2. Search for "SecureConnect" and click Install.
  3. Once installation completes, launch SecureConnect from the system tray.
  4. Sign in with your company email, e.g. firstname.lastname@technova.example.com.
  5. Complete multifactor authentication when prompted.
  6. Select the "TechNova-Corporate" connection profile and click Connect.
  7. A green shield icon in the system tray confirms the VPN is active.

4. INSTALLATION ON MACOS

  1. Open Self Service from the Applications folder.
  2. Search for "SecureConnect" and click Install.
  3. Launch SecureConnect from the menu bar.
  4. Sign in with your company email and complete multifactor authentication.
  5. Select the "TechNova-Corporate" connection profile and click Connect.
  6. A green lock icon in the menu bar confirms the VPN is active.

5. CONNECTING FROM A REMOTE LOCATION

Always connect to SecureConnect before accessing internal tools such as the
HR intranet, shared drives, or any customer data, especially when using
public or home Wi-Fi. Do not use split tunneling unless your role has been
explicitly approved for it by IT Security; approved roles are configured
automatically and do not require manual setup.

6. MULTIFACTOR AUTHENTICATION (MFA) ENROLLMENT

  1. Go to identity.technova.example.com from any browser.
  2. Sign in with your company email and temporary password provided by IT
     during onboarding.
  3. Choose your MFA method: the TechNova Authenticator app (recommended) or
     SMS text message.
  4. Scan the QR code with the Authenticator app, or enter your mobile number
     for SMS codes.
  5. Enter the 6-digit verification code to confirm enrollment.

7. TROUBLESHOOTING

Connection fails immediately after clicking Connect:
  - Confirm your laptop's date and time are set automatically; an incorrect
    clock will cause authentication to fail.
  - Restart SecureConnect and try again.

"Authentication failed" error:
  - Confirm you are using your full company email address, not just your
    username.
  - Reset your password through the company password portal if you are
    unsure it is current.

VPN connects but internal sites do not load:
  - Disconnect and reconnect SecureConnect.
  - Confirm the "TechNova-Corporate" profile is selected, not "TechNova-Guest".
  - Restart your laptop if the issue persists after reconnecting.

Lost or new phone, cannot receive MFA codes:
  - Contact the IT Help Desk to temporarily disable MFA so you can re-enroll
    a new device. Identity verification will be required over a company
    Zoom call with camera on.

8. FORGOTTEN PASSWORDS

For forgotten passwords, use the company password portal at
password.technova.example.com, or call the IT Help Desk at extension 4400.
The IT Help Desk is available Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM
Eastern Time. For after-hours emergencies affecting production systems,
call the on-call pager line listed on the IT intranet page.

9. SECURITY REMINDERS

Never share your VPN credentials or MFA codes with anyone, including other
TechNova employees or IT staff; IT will never ask for your MFA code over
phone or email. Report any suspicious login prompts or unexpected MFA
requests to security@technova.example.com immediately.
