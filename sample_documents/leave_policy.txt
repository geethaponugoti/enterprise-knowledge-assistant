TechNova Solutions Leave Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Document Owner: Human Resources Department

1. PURPOSE

This policy describes the paid and unpaid leave benefits available to
TechNova Solutions employees. It applies to all full-time employees; part-time
employees accrue leave on a prorated basis according to their scheduled
hours.

2. VACATION LEAVE

Full-time employees receive 15 paid vacation days per calendar year, accrued
monthly at a rate of 1.25 days per month. Vacation time may be used as soon as
it is accrued; there is no waiting period for new hires, but new employees may
not take more than 5 days of vacation within their first 90 days without
manager approval.

A maximum of five unused vacation days may be carried into the next calendar
year. Any vacation balance above five days that remains unused as of December
31 is forfeited, except where prohibited by local law. Employees in
California and other states with use-it-or-lose-it restrictions instead
accrue vacation with no annual cap, per state requirements.

3. SICK LEAVE

Full-time employees receive 10 paid sick days per calendar year, available in
full at the start of each year. Sick leave may be used for the employee's own
illness, medical appointments, or to care for an immediate family member.
Unused sick leave does not carry over to the following year and is not paid
out upon termination.

Employees who are absent for more than three consecutive sick days must
provide a doctor's note to HR before returning to work.

4. PAID HOLIDAYS

TechNova observes 11 paid holidays each year in the United States:
  - New Year's Day
  - Martin Luther King Jr. Day
  - Memorial Day
  - Juneteenth
  - Independence Day
  - Labor Day
  - Thanksgiving Day
  - Day after Thanksgiving
  - Christmas Eve
  - Christmas Day
  - New Year's Eve

Employees in the Dublin office observe the applicable Irish public holiday
schedule instead, published annually by HR in December.

5. PARENTAL LEAVE

Employees who have completed at least 6 months of continuous service are
eligible for 12 weeks of paid parental leave following the birth, adoption,
or foster placement of a child. Parental leave must be taken within 12 months
of the qualifying event and may be split into two blocks with manager
approval. Parental leave runs concurrently with any leave required under
applicable federal or state law, such as the Family and Medical Leave Act.

6. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE

Employees may take up to 5 paid days of bereavement leave upon the death of
an immediate family member (spouse, child, parent, or sibling), and up to 2
paid days for an extended family member. Additional unpaid leave may be
approved by a manager on a case-by-case basis.

7. JURY DUTY AND VOTING LEAVE

TechNova provides paid leave for jury duty for the full duration of service,
provided the employee gives HR a copy of the jury summons in advance.
Employees also receive up to 3 hours of paid leave to vote in any public
election if their work schedule does not otherwise allow sufficient time to
vote.

8. UNPAID LEAVE OF ABSENCE

Employees who have exhausted their paid leave balances may request an unpaid
leave of absence of up to 30 days, subject to manager and HR approval. Longer
unpaid leave requests are evaluated individually and may affect benefits
eligibility.

9. HOW TO REQUEST LEAVE

All leave requests must be submitted through the WorkClock system at least 5
business days in advance for planned leave. For unplanned absences, such as
sudden illness, employees must notify their manager as early as possible on
the day of the absence, and log the leave in WorkClock within 2 business days
of returning to work.

10. QUESTIONS

Direct any questions about this policy to hr@technova.example.com or your HR
business partner.
